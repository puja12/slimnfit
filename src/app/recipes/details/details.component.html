Pumpkin and Chickpea pizza with prawns

  • Time 25 mins
  • Serving 3 people

Ingredients :

  1. 1 fresh long red chilli, deseeded, finely chopped
  2. 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  3. 250g peeled green prawns, tails intact
  4. 500g peeled pumpkin, finely grated
  5. 120g (1 cup) chickpea (besan) flour
  6. 1 tbsp chia seeds
  7. 2 tbsp finely grated parmesan
  8. 1 tsp dried oregano leaves
  9. 1 egg
  10. 80ml (1/3 cup) Mutti Tomato Passata
  11. 100g baby spinach leaves
  12. 1 zucchini, peeled into ribbons
  13. 150g grape tomatoes, halved
  14. 30g creamy feta, crumbled
  15. 1 tsp finely grated lemon rin

Method

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 200C/180C fan forced. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper. Combine chilli and oil in a shallow glass or ceramic dish. add prawns to stir to coat place in fridge for 20 minuts to marinate
  • Step 2: Meanwhile, place the pumpkin in a sieve. Squeeze to remove excess liquid. Combine the pumpkin, chickpea flour, chia seeds, parmesan, oregano and egg in a large bowl.
  • Step 3: Spread pumpkin mixture into 2 rectangles (about 20 x 24cm) on the prepared trays. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden and set.
  • Step 4: Spread the pumpkin bases with passata. Top with half the spinach leaves, then the zucchini, tomato and prawns. Sprinkle with feta and rind. Bake for 10 minutes or until the prawns are cooked through. Top with the remaining spinach and serve with lemon.
